Comparative Politics

Talks 2018

André Walter

Tags: Swiss Politics; History; Comparative Politics

There are a couple of upcoming talks and conferences in 2018 where I’ll present some of my ongoing work. I’m going to cover the following topics at the ECPR im Hamburg, the DVPW in Frankfurt a.M., the ESPAnet in Vilnius and the Political Science Department Seminar in Tübingen:

  • A Socialist Threat? Radical Party Entry, Electoral Competition, and the Formation of Alliances in Majoritarian Systems (Tübingen; ECPR; DVPW)

  • The Social Origins of Christian Democracy. Rural-Urban Migration and the Rise of the Catholic Workers’ Movement (ESPAnet in Vilnius)

  • Income Tax, Indirect Revenue, and Political Institutions. Direct Democracy and the Creation of the Early Tax State (ECPR in Hamburg; DVPW in Frankfurt a.M.)

About the Author

André Walter is a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Department of Political Science, University of St. Gallen.
