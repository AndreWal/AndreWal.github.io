I’m currently working with Patrick Emmenegger on creating a database on the ideological composition of cantonal governments. We were able to compile a time series for most cantons starting in the mid 19th century. A complete Time-Series Cross-Section is available for all cantons for 1894 onwards (except Appenzell Inner-Rhodes which starts 1928 and, of course, Jura).
